## Quick Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    ````
    Go to your Raxmatullo Erkinov dashboard and choose your project.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    ````
    Press the "Add Page" button at the top.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    ````
    Give your page a title, then write or paste your content.
    Save when finished.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
